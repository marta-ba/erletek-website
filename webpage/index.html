Our Journey

ERLEtek is a technology company that integrates expertise in construction, software, and robotics. Our mission is to enable automation in construction technologies. To begin our journey, we have started focusing on simple concrete works such as land retaining walls with agile mobile 3D concrete printers for rapid, on-site construction - empowering construction projects with speed, precision, adaptability and open new possibilities for architectural design.

VC-One

Our first product, VC-ONE, is a mobile 3D concrete printer designed for on-site use, enabling faster and more flexible construction. It features quick setup, adaptability to varying terrain, and the ability to print both curved and straight structures. With swarm intelligence, the vision is to have multiple units that can collaborate like a team of robots for large-scale projects. The VC-ONE supports a wide range of materials and includes digital controls with real-time monitoring. Its compact, modular design makes it scalable and ideal for modern, efficient construction workflows.
